SPECTAPRIME

No profiling, still loans in 5 minutes!

SpectaPrime; gives you loans in 5 minutes with no profiling needed. It simply helps you borrow against your existing savings, cash deposits or investments for your personal needs or urgent business needs.

These savings and investment will not be depleted but will still earn you some interest.

Benefits
  • SpectaPrime helps you have savings available for emergencies.
  • SpectaPrime helps you secure a line of credit with your savings or investments if you have seasonal cash flow fluctuations. It gives you low interest rate and there are no demands for your inflow.
  • You can pledge your personal savings or investment to a corporate debt, facilitating approval and lowering the cost of credit.
  • SpectaPrime protects you against rising interest rates.

New Investment

This option enables you to invest your existing savings (NGN, USD, EURO, GBP) in fixed deposit with a maximum of one-year investment option for loans and maximum of five-year investment for credit cards; and apply for a loan or credit card to match the investment tenor.

It gives you loans of up to 83% of the investment amount with a lower loan interest rate in comparison to conventional rates charged by Banks. For example, for every N100 in your account, you can borrow up to N83.

Existing Investment

This option enables you to use any of your existing investments with Sterling Bank (fixed deposit, SRDA, Doubble, DCD, Treasury Bills) in these currencies: NGN, USD, EURO, GBP to apply for a loan for which tenor will not exceed the remaining tenor of the identified investment.

Eligible loan tenor must not be less than 7 days. This implies that where the remaining tenor of an existing Investment as at the time of loan application is less than 7 days, you will not be able to use such investment to take a loan.
